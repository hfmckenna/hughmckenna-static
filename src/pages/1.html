TART

Non traditional tart recipe.

PREP: 1 hour 30 mins
COOK: 30 mins
YIELD: 8 Inch Tart
INGREDIENTS

PASTRY

  • 175g White Flour
  • 50g Sugar
  • 75g Ground Almonds
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 100g fridge cold butter

FILLING

  • 100g Sultanas
  • 100 Walnuts
  • 50g room temperature Butter
  • 1 egg
  • 50g Icing Sugar
  • Tablespoon good, fruity vinegar. Dessert wine vinegar or cider vinegar.

METHOD

01

Autolyse

Add flour and almonds to a bowl, crumble in the cold butter.

02

Inoculation & Salt

Add sugar, egg and enough cold water to form the pastry.

03

Bulk Fermentation

Rest pastry in the fridge.

Note: An hour should do it.
04

Bake

Partially grind walnuts, roughly half chunky, half ground.

05

Bake

Mix sultanas, walnuts, icing sugar, egg and room temperature butter. Then add the vinegar to taste.

Note: The vinegar should add balance, depth and a separate fruity note.
06

Bake

Roll the pastry, line the tart case, blind bake and allow to cool.

07

Bake

Add filling to tart case and bake for around 20 minutes at medium heat.
